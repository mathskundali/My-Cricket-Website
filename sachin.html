International Debut

Sachin made his Test debut for India at the age of just 16 against Pakistan in 1989. Facing some of the fiercest fast bowlers in the world, he displayed remarkable courage. His ODI debut followed soon after.

These early performances signaled the arrival of a future legend who would redefine cricket history forever.

Batting Style & Technique

Sachin Tendulkar was known for his perfect balance, impeccable timing, and technical mastery. His straight drive is considered one of the most beautiful shots in cricket.

He combined classical technique with modern aggression, making him effective in both Test cricket and limited-overs formats.
