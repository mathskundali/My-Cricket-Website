Bowling Accuracy & Control

The ultimate cricket guide to mastering precision, consistency, swing, seam, spin and mental discipline.

What is Bowling Accuracy?

Bowling accuracy is the ability to repeatedly land the ball on a desired line and length. It creates pressure, limits scoring and forces mistakes. Control allows bowlers to execute tactical plans regardless of pitch or conditions.

Line, Length & Control Zones

  • Off-stump corridor dominance
  • Good length consistency
  • Full-length swing control
  • Short-ball surprise accuracy

Biomechanics & Body Control

Stable run-up, strong front arm, upright head position and a repeatable release point are essential for elite accuracy.

Accuracy Drills

  • Target cone bowling
  • Six-ball same-spot challenge
  • Reduced run-up control drill
  • Pressure overs simulation

Mental Discipline

Elite bowlers visualize the target, trust their action, and execute without fear. Accuracy thrives on patience and confidence.
